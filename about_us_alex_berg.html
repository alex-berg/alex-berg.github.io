Autobiography

Sports nutritionist Alex Berg

  • 1975, 12 years old - youth team of the Kyrgyz SSR in swimming.
  • 1976, 13 years old - Champion of the Kyrgyz SSR in swimming. 1 adult

  • 1979, 16 years old - transferred to the Kyrgyz national water polo team.

Holding trainings and seminars on a healthy lifestyle. Consultations on the organization of proper nutrition in medical and spot institutions.
